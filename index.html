My Poems

Title of Poem 1

Our minds blur at the edges
your thoughts, my thoughts, a single pulse. Time, a living thread,
moving slowly through the strings, the universe hums. A distant sun rises, black trees casting shadows on us, while we are drifting through the golden beams. by J. W.

Title of Poem 2


It's almost like I hear you saying, "So, what do you want?" "I want you," I said in my mind, but to you, I said, "Nothing." And so you left,
thinking that this is what I meant to say. by Julie Webster

Title of Poem 3

"Give it time," you said,

As if time was gentle, As if it did not carve through me

like wind through withering branches. by Julie Webster
